What on earth is Drawbacks!?!

Drawbacks is a 2015 entry for js13kgames. Based on the theme of 'Reversed', you have to develop a HTML5 game in a month with only 13kbs to play with. Introducing Drawbacks... A game were you require quick thinking in order to not follow the rules... Whatever the game tells you to do, do the complete opposite!
